Tadesse Desta is a prominent coffee exporter in Ethiopia, specializing in the export of high-quality Arabica coffee to 16 different countries across the Middle East, Europe, and Asia. In the past year (2015 EC), the company's coffee exports reached 2,800 MT, demonstrating a consistent growth in market presence over the last five years. Tadesse Desta sources its coffee from 9 different sites, including Birbirsa Kojowa, Dimtu, Buku saysa, Buku Abel, Benti Nenka, Bishan Fugu, Foge, Dekitu, and Yirgacheffe Chelchelle, all located in West Guji and Gedeo
